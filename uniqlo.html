Adding Fun to the Everyday

Introducing the first UNIQLO x ANYA HINDMARCH collection. The innovative British brand is known for championing creativity and craftsmanship, while always adding a touch of fun.

Posted by S. P. on November 18, 2023

Anya Hindmarch founded her business in London in 1987 and has since grown into a global brand. It is known for its luxury, organization-obsessed accessories, and playful, experiential retail concepts. Creativity, modern craftsmanship, and personalization are at the heart of Anya Hindmarch's work.

This capsule collection reimagines UNIQLO's knitwear via carefully crafted deconstructed details, playfully overseen by the brand’s iconic Eyes.

A timeless sweater is updated with a combination of stitching techniques and external seaming, elevating a classic silhouette, while HEATTECH beanies and scarves blend contrast stitches and unexpected elements – each piece bringing a playful note to the everyday. Every design features the much-loved Eyes motif in considered positions, occasionally with the addition of a stitched mouth—the final note that celebrates exemplary attention to detail.

This capsule collection brings Anya Hindmarch’s creative perspective to the modern wardrobe. The result is considered, effortless pieces with distinct details and a dose of attitude - Adding Fun to the Everyday.
